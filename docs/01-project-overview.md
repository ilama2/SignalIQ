# SignalIQ

## Overview

SignalIQ is an AI-powered investment intelligence platform focused on the Saudi stock market (Tadawul) with future support for GCC and global markets.

The platform is designed to help investors make better investment decisions by combining:

- Financial statements
- Company information
- Live market data
- Market news
- AI analysis
- Technical indicators
- Fundamental analysis
- Investment recommendations
- Portfolio analytics

SignalIQ targets investors of all experience levels by presenting complex financial information in an intuitive interface while providing advanced tools for experienced investors.

---

## Problem

Investors often need to navigate multiple platforms to access market data, company financials, news, technical analysis, and investment insights. This fragmentation consumes time and increases the likelihood of overlooking critical information. Additionally, there is a lack of Arabic platforms that integrate these services with modern AI capabilities specifically tailored to the Saudi market.

---

## Vision

Build the Bloomberg Terminal for Saudi retail investors.

The platform should become the single source of truth for investment decisions.
